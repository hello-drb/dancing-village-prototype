Common questions

Things people often
wonder before reaching out.

Do I need to have a fully formed partnership strategy before I reach out? +

Not at all. Some of the most valuable work Tammy does is helping organizations figure out their strategy in the first place. If you know you need corporate partnerships but aren't sure where to start, that's exactly the right moment to have a conversation.

What size organizations do you typically work with? +

Dancing Village works with nonprofits, media companies, cultural institutions, mission-led businesses, and purpose-driven founders — any organization with a meaningful initiative that needs corporate partners and funding behind it. What matters more than sector or size is whether the mission is real and the leadership is ready to commit to the work.

How long does an engagement typically last? +

It depends on the scope. A project-based engagement might run 3–6 months. A retainer arrangement is often ongoing. Advisory relationships can be quarterly check-ins. Tammy structures every engagement around what your organization actually needs — not a predetermined timeline.

Do you work internationally? +

Yes. Tammy works with organizations around the world. While her network is deepest in the US market, she has experience building international partnerships and working across different sectors and geographies.

What does it cost to work with you? +

Pricing is tailored to the scope and structure of each engagement. Tammy doesn't publish rates because the right structure varies too much to be meaningful out of context. The first conversation is always free — and if there's a fit, she'll share a clear, transparent proposal.
